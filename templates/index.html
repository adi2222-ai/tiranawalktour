Why Visit Albania?

Albania is Europe's best-kept secret, offering incredible history, stunning landscapes, and warm hospitality at unbeatable prices.

Skanderbeg Square

The heart of Tirana, surrounded by important government buildings and cultural institutions.

Bunk'Art Museums

Fascinating underground bunkers turned into multimedia museums showcasing Albania's communist past.

Et'hem Bey Mosque

Beautiful 18th-century Ottoman mosque with intricate frescoes and peaceful courtyard.

Dajti Mountain

Take the cable car up for breathtaking panoramic views of the city and Adriatic coast.

National History Museum

Albania's largest museum featuring artifacts from prehistoric times to the present day.

Culinary Delights

Taste authentic Albanian cuisine influenced by Ottoman, Italian, and Mediterranean traditions.
